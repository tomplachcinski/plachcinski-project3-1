Step into the captivating world of King’s Landing, the legendary capital of the Seven Kingdoms from Game of Thrones. Explore iconic landmarks like the Red Keep and the bustling markets of Flea Bottom. Immerse yourself in the city’s rich history, where every cobblestone street echoes tales of power and betrayal. Witness the drama unfold at the Great Sept of Baelor and indulge in the decadent cuisine. Join us in the heart of Westeros, where the game of thrones takes center stage.
