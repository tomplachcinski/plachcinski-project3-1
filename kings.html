Explore

Kings Landing

Step into the captivating world of King’s Landing, the legendary capital of the Seven Kingdoms from Game of Thrones. Explore iconic landmarks like the Red Keep and the bustling markets of Flea Bottom. Immerse yourself in the city’s rich history, where every cobblestone street echoes tales of power and betrayal. Witness the drama unfold at the Great Sept of Baelor and indulge in the decadent cuisine. Join us in the heart of Westeros, where the game of thrones takes center stage. Welcome to King’s Landing – where legends are born and destinies are forged.

Buy Some Armor

In King's Landing, buying armor is a mix of negotiation and discernment. The markets offer diverse styles, from traditional to ornate. Haggling is crucial, and inspecting craftsmanship is key for durability in the unpredictable politics of the Seven Kingdoms.

Practice Archery

In King's Landing, archery enthusiasts of all backgrounds practice their skills at ranges scattered throughout the city. The twang of bowstrings fills the air as individuals exchange tips, guided by seasoned instructors, in a shared pursuit of mastery.

Read Our History

In King's Landing, history comes alive in the Citadel and local libraries, where scholars unravel Westerosi lore. Maesters and tutors share captivating tales of wars and dynasties, making the pursuit of historical knowledge engaging for all.

Meet Our King

Meeting the king in King's Landing is a formal affair in the opulent Red Keep's throne room. Courtiers navigate etiquette in a delicate dance of protocol and diplomacy, bearing both awe and political significance in the ruler's presence.
